Salad

Salad Bowl

Ingredients

  • 4 big tomatoes
  • 2 cucumbers
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • lemon juice according to personal preference
  • 1 big Onion
  • Pepper mix
  • parsley, finely chopped
  • Dried mint
  • Salt
  • Sumac
  • Pomegranate Molasses

Steps

  1. First, gather your ingredients for the dressing. Add them all to a mason jar and shake to combine. You'll end up with a dark brown dressing.
  2. gather your vegetables and wash them
  3. Chop the vegetables finely. Each vegetable should be roughly 1/4 of an inch or less in size.
  4. Add all the vegetables to a bowl and mix to combine. Season them with a pinch of salt. Pour over some of the dressing. For this quantity of salad, I usually add about 1/4 cup of dressing, mix and taste, then add more as needed.
