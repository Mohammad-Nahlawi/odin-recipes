Tea

A cup of green tea

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of water
  • Your preferred kind tea leaves or teabags
  • Your preferred quantity of sugar

Steps

  1. Boil the water
  2. Add the tea leaves/teabag
  3. wait for around 5 minutes, until the tea get well-soaked in the water
  4. Pour the tea in a cup and add sugar, Enjoy!
